The 50-day Simple Moving Average (50 SMA) is a key technical indicator used to identify the medium-term trend and act as a dynamic level of support or resistance. As a lagging indicator, traders must be cautious of false signals in choppy markets and should always use it in conjunction with other analytical tools for confirmation.
